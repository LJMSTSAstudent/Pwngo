Ingredients:

  • 1 lb chicken breast fillets (cut into strips)
  • ½ cup flour (for dredging)
  • 2 large eggs (beaten)
  • 1 cup breadcrumbs (panko for extra crunch)
  • ½ tsp garlic powder
  • ½ tsp paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Oil for frying (preferably canola oil)

Instructions:

Getting the Chicken Ready:

  1. Start by cutting your chicken breast fillets into nice, even strips. Once cut, pat them dry with paper towels.

Setting Up Your Dredging Stations:

  1. Prepare three shallow dishes. In the first one, place flour seasoned with salt and pepper.
  2. In the second dish, beat two eggs until well mixed.
  3. For the third dish, combine breadcrumbs with garlic powder and paprika.

Breading the Chicken:

  1. Coat each chicken strip first in the flour, shaking off any excess.
  2. Next, dip it into the beaten eggs, ensuring full coverage.
  3. Finally, press the strip into the breadcrumb mixture to coat evenly.

Cooking:

  1. Heat about ½ inch of oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat (350°F).
  2. Fry the breaded chicken strips in batches for 4-5 minutes per side until golden brown.
  3. Drain on paper towels and serve immediately with your favorite dipping sauces.

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices artisan bread (sourdough or whole grain)
  • 1 ripe avocado
  • ½ cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • ¼ cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1 tbsp balsamic glaze
  • 1 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Red pepper flakes (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Toast the bread slices until golden and crisp.
  2. Cut the avocado in half, remove the pit, and scoop the flesh into a bowl.
  3. Mash the avocado with a fork and season with salt and pepper.
  4. Spread the mashed avocado evenly on the toasted bread.
  5. Top with cherry tomatoes and crumbled feta.
  6. Drizzle with olive oil and balsamic glaze.
  7. Sprinkle with red pepper flakes if desired and serve immediately.

Ingredients:

  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp ginger, grated
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 1 cup snap peas
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 1 tsp cornstarch mixed with 2 tbsp water
  • Sesame seeds for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Heat oil in a wok or large skillet over high heat.
  2. Add garlic and ginger, stir for 30 seconds until fragrant.
  3. Add the harder vegetables (carrots, broccoli) first and stir-fry for 2 minutes.
  4. Add remaining vegetables and stir-fry for another 3 minutes.
  5. Add soy sauce, oyster sauce, and sesame oil, tossing to coat.
  6. Pour in cornstarch mixture and stir until sauce thickens.
  7. Garnish with sesame seeds and serve over rice or noodles.
