Crispy Chicken Tenders

By James Smith | 25 mins

Description

Golden-brown chicken tenders with a crunchy exterior and juicy interior. Perfect with your favorite dipping sauce!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb chicken breast fillets (cut into strips)
  • ½ cup flour (for dredging)
  • 2 large eggs (beaten)
  • 1 cup breadcrumbs (panko for extra crunch)
  • ½ tsp garlic powder
  • ½ tsp paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Oil for frying (preferably canola oil)

Instructions

  1. Prepare the chicken: Cut chicken breast into even strips and pat dry with paper towels.
  2. Set up dredging stations: Place flour in first bowl, beaten eggs in second, and breadcrumbs mixed with seasonings in third.
  3. Bread the chicken: Coat each strip in flour, then egg, then breadcrumb mixture, pressing gently to adhere.
  4. Heat oil: Pour ½ inch of oil in skillet and heat to 350°F (medium-high heat).
  5. Fry the tenders: Cook in batches for 4-5 minutes per side until golden brown and internal temperature reaches 165°F.
  6. Drain and serve: Transfer to paper towel-lined plate to drain excess oil. Serve immediately with dipping sauces.

Tips

  • For extra crispiness, double coat by repeating the egg and breadcrumb steps
  • Try adding ¼ cup grated parmesan to the breadcrumbs for extra flavor
  • Bake at 400°F for 15-20 minutes for a healthier version

Gourmet Avocado Toast

By Maria Garcia | 10 mins

Description

Creamy avocado on toasted artisan bread with cherry tomatoes, feta, and a drizzle of balsamic glaze.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices artisan bread (sourdough or whole grain)
  • 1 ripe avocado
  • ½ cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • ¼ cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1 tbsp balsamic glaze
  • 1 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Red pepper flakes (optional)

Instructions

  1. Toast the bread: Lightly toast bread slices until golden and crisp.
  2. Prepare avocado: Cut avocado in half, remove pit, and scoop flesh into bowl. Mash with fork and season with salt and pepper.
  3. Assemble: Spread mashed avocado evenly on toasted bread.
  4. Add toppings: Top with cherry tomatoes and crumbled feta cheese.
  5. Finish: Drizzle with olive oil and balsamic glaze. Sprinkle with red pepper flakes if desired.
  6. Serve immediately while bread is still warm and crisp.

Variations

  • Add a poached egg for extra protein
  • Substitute goat cheese for feta for a creamier texture
  • Add microgreens or arugula for freshness

Speedy Vegetable Stir Fry

By Chen Wei | 20 mins

Description

Colorful veggies in a savory garlic-ginger sauce, ready in minutes. Serve over rice or noodles.

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp ginger, grated
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 1 cup snap peas
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 1 tsp cornstarch mixed with 2 tbsp water
  • Sesame seeds for garnish

Instructions

  1. Prep vegetables: Wash and cut all vegetables into uniform sizes for even cooking.
  2. Heat wok: Heat oil in wok or large skillet over high heat until shimmering.
  3. Aromatics: Add garlic and ginger, stir for 30 seconds until fragrant.
  4. Cook hard vegetables: Add carrots and broccoli first, stir-fry for 2 minutes.
  5. Add remaining veggies: Add bell pepper and snap peas, stir-fry for another 3 minutes.
  6. Add sauces: Pour in soy sauce, oyster sauce, and sesame oil, tossing to coat evenly.
  7. Thicken sauce: Add cornstarch mixture and stir until sauce thickens and coats vegetables.
  8. Garnish and serve: Sprinkle with sesame seeds and serve immediately over rice or noodles.

Pro Tips

  • Have all ingredients prepped before starting as stir-fry cooks quickly
  • Keep the heat high to prevent vegetables from becoming soggy
  • Add protein like tofu, chicken, or shrimp for a complete meal

Toasty Tomato Soup

By Geronimo Wilson | 1.5 hours

Description

Roasted seasoned pureed tomatoes with smoked paprika and garlic chives. Serve with cream and basil for extra taste.

Ingredients

  • 3 lbs ripe tomatoes, halved
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Arrange tomatoes, onion and garlic on baking sheet. Drizzle with olive oil.
  3. Roast for 45 minutes until vegetables are soft and slightly charred.
  4. Transfer to blender, add paprika and broth. Blend until smooth.
  5. Strain through sieve into pot, simmer for 20 minutes.
  6. Stir in cream, season with salt and pepper.
  7. Garnish with fresh basil before serving.

Pumpkin Pie

By Jessica Monroe | 1.5 hours

Description

This creamy pumpkin filling with a crunchy, golden, flaky outer crust.

Ingredients

  • 1 pie crust
  • 15 oz pumpkin puree
  • 14 oz sweetened condensed milk
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp ginger
  • 1/2 tsp nutmeg
  • 1/2 tsp salt

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. Mix pumpkin puree, condensed milk, eggs, spices and salt in bowl.
  3. Pour into pie crust.
  4. Bake for 15 minutes, then reduce temperature to 350°F (175°C).
  5. Bake for additional 35-40 minutes until center is set.
  6. Cool completely before serving.

Slow-Cooked Beef Stew

By Nelson Uganda | 2 hours

Description

Beef stew has tender beef, carrots, and potatoes in a rich, savory broth. Add peas, celery, or parsnips for extra flavor and texture.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs beef chuck, cubed
  • 2 tbsp flour
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 carrots, sliced
  • 3 potatoes, cubed
  • 4 cups beef broth
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tsp thyme
  • 1 bay leaf
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Toss beef cubes with flour, salt and pepper.
  2. Heat oil in Dutch oven, brown beef in batches.
  3. Add onion, cook until soft.
  4. Return beef to pot, add remaining ingredients.
  5. Bring to boil, then reduce heat to low.
  6. Simmer covered for 1.5-2 hours until beef is tender.
  7. Remove bay leaf before serving.

Cheesy Jalapeno Poppers

By Tina Gomez | 45 mins

Description

Spicy jalapenos stuffed with cream cheese and cheddar, wrapped in crispy breadcrumbs.

Ingredients

  • 12 jalapeno peppers
  • 8 oz cream cheese
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 cup breadcrumbs
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • Oil for frying

Instructions

  1. Cut jalapenos in half lengthwise, remove seeds.
  2. Mix cream cheese, cheddar and garlic powder.
  3. Fill jalapeno halves with cheese mixture.
  4. Dip in egg, then breadcrumbs.
  5. Fry in 350°F oil for 2-3 minutes until golden.
  6. Drain on paper towels before serving.

Spicy Mango Avocado Tacos

By Hernando Cucho De Stefano | 45 mins

Description

Spicy Mango Avocado Tacos are a fresh, vibrant dish with sweet mango, creamy avocado, crunchy veggies, and a zesty lime dressing, perfect for a light meal or appetizer.

Ingredients

  • 8 small corn tortillas
  • 2 ripe avocados, sliced
  • 1 mango, diced
  • 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup shredded purple cabbage
  • 1/4 cup chopped cilantro
  • 1 jalapeno, sliced
  • 2 limes, juiced
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp honey
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Whisk together lime juice, olive oil, honey and salt for dressing.
  2. Warm tortillas according to package directions.
  3. Assemble tacos with avocado, mango, onion, cabbage and jalapeno.
  4. Drizzle with dressing and garnish with cilantro.
  5. Serve immediately.
