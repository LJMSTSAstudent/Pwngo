Our Story

SpaceTime Online Kitchen was founded in 2007 in Richmond, VA by a group of 5 passionate chefs. Their mission was simple: educate as many people as possible about culinary arts. Starting with local classes, they soon recognized the power of digital platforms to expand their reach. Today, we serve hundreds of users daily through our unique online cooking platform, community outreach programs, and charitable initiatives.
